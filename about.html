About ME

Fashion Designer and also a Medical Laboratory Scientist

My love and passion for Fashion came form the world and people around me. It allows me to express my creative side. For me fashion is more than just clothing. It's an art form that speaks to our identity, our culture, and our values. It's a way to express ourselves and connect with others. Fashion is about taking risks, pushing boundaries, and creating something new and exciting. It's about daring to be different and standing out in a sea of sameness. It's a celebration of self-expression and individuality
